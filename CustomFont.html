Custom Font

Original font that I created by combining three different font

Process

I started with playing around with the shape of each letter. I used the Broadway font as the base of each character so each letter will have that distinct thick vertical lines and thin horizontal lines.

I used the top and bottom parts of the font mixed with the base font and condensed it to give the new font a mix of all three font. The Old English font had that characteristic where there is more than 1 stroke to form the letter. I implemented the white curved stripes to form some separation within the letters and make them a bit more distinct.

Rationale

For this project, I decided to use the phrase “Seize The Day”. The three font that I focused on were Onyx, Broadway, and Old English. Each font that I chose were unique in their way. Onyx was a condense and stretched font with very sharp serifs; Broadway had thick strokes on vertical lines and thin strokes on horizontal lines and lastly Old English has interesting curved line details inside the capital letters as well as it was oddly sharp with it’s lower case characters.

For my design, I went with Broadway as the base and then made the top half more Onyx focus where I took the characteristics of the sharp serifs and the bottom half focused more towards the Old English. The overall shape was sharp like Old English lowercase letters as well as I took the idea of implementing the curved designs from the capital letter.
