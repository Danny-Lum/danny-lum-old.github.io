Net Neutrality

A series of small projects dedicated towards net neutrality

On December 14, 2017, the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) voted in favor of repealing the neutrality policies that were placed back in 2005. This collection was to raise awareness of the harm that it can affect consumers if net neutrality was approved

Event Poster

Posters and digital mockups for a made up event for the Canada Aviation and Space Museum.

A Flight Through History is a made-up event that showcases the different spaces and exhibitions that the museum has to offer. This project consists of 3 poster variations, a computer, tablet, and phone mockup, and brochure design for the event.
