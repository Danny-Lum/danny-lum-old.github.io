Net Neutrality

A series of small projects dedicated towards net neutrality

Rationale

The idea behind this project is to raise awareness of Net Neutrality and the possible effects it can cause in the online industry. This project contains 3 different mini-projects that help promote the idea of supporting Net Neutrality and avoid losing it.

Poster

The first of this series is a poster. This poster shows the logo of twitter caged inside a computer screen having its eyes censored out with a black bar. This was to represent the idea that if Net Neutrality were to be taken away, the privacy of information can be limited as well as our rights on the internet could change for the worse.



Magazine Spread

The second project from this series is a spread for a computer focused magazine. The structure is based on an existing magazine and was redesigned to fit this project. The main cover spread was to give a sense of commonality where people of all ages can relate to, the offline google page and have that as a common theme through my second spread.




Bus & Instagram Ad

The last and final piece for this project is a combination of a bus and an Instagram ad. The main focus of this was the demographic that I was going for. I was primarily targeting young teens and adults who commute often.
